About Peplepay

Peplepay is a modern digital payments company built using the Pulsepoint NG platform. We are under PointNG Digital Service BN37473 and committed to providing fast, secure, and reliable solutions for your everyday payments.

With Peplepay, you can easily recharge data and airtime, pay bills, manage payouts, and use virtual cards—all from one seamless platform. Our mission is to simplify digital transactions in Nigeria and beyond.
